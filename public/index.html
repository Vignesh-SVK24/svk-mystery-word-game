🔮 How to Play

🎭 The Roles

🔪 Killer (1)

Receives a riddle or sentence that hints at the secret word. Your goal is to blend in and mislead others.

🔍 Detective (0-1)

Unlocked at 5+ players. Can see a suspect list and help lead the discussion to find the killer.

👤 Guests (Rest)

You receive the secret word as scrambled tiles. Unscramble it to join the meeting!

🎯 Victory Conditions

  • Guests Win: Successfully identify and kick the Killer out.
  • Killer Wins: Reduce the number of alive players to 3.

🔄 Game Flow

  1. Reveal: Get your role and secret clue/tiles.
  2. Round: Guests unscramble words while chatting. Anyone can call a meeting once they're "Ready".
  3. Meeting: Everyone discusses the mystery. Cast your votes on who is most suspicious.
  4. Kick: The player with the most votes is removed. Their role is revealed!
